Moon: Remix RPG Adventure

Don't be a hero. Experience the love. Experience moon.

Often credited as one of the first 'anti-rpgs', Moon is a Japanese RPG that breaks common RPG conventions by going against the architypical 'kill every monster you come across' style of RPG goals. Instead, Moon is about love and saving the souls of enemies.
